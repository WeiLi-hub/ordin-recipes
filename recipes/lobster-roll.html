How To Make Lobster Roll

Description

Lobster is tasty, but the boston lobster roll is more funny thing that need to try and enjoy

After check out this recipe, you can makes a fancy luncheon to impress your friends in this wondeful summer

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter, softened
  • 4 roll (blank)s hot dog buns or kaiser rolls, split
  • 4 large leaf (blank)s lettuce leaves
  • 1.5 pounds cooked and cubed lobster meat
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 dash hot pepper sauce (e.g. Tabascoâ„¢)
  • 2 medium (4-1/8" long)s green onions, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch dried basil, parsley or tarragon

Steps

  1. Lightly butter the insides of the buns or rolls and line with lettuce leaves. Set aside
  2. Stir together the mayonnaise, lime juice, hot pepper sauce, salt and pepper
  3. Stuff the lobster filling into the buns and sprinkle parsley, basil or tarragon lightly over the filling
