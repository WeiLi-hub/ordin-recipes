How To Make Ramen

Description

This flavorful bowl of ramen in miso broth is full of delicious flavors that will definitely make your palate sing!

If you've been dreaming of Japan, this is the perfect meal for you.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) beef sirloin steak
  • 0.5 cup gluten-free soy sauce (tamari)
  • 1 teaspoon coconut oil
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 teaspoons miso paste
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages ramen noodles
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. marinate steak and soy sauce for two hours
  2. heat coconut oil with marinated steak
  3. combine broth, miso paste, garlic, and sesame oil in a saucepan over medium heat
  4. cook ramen noodles for 15 minutes
  5. combine broth and noodles, finished!!
